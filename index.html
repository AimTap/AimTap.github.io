🧠 What Is Reaction Time?

Reaction time is the interval between a stimulus (green screen) and your response. Lower milliseconds = faster reflexes. Elite gamers score under 180ms. This test uses random delays (1-4s) to prevent anticipation.

📊 Grading Scale (Reaction Test)

S: <180ms · A: 180–230ms · B: 230–300ms · C: 300ms+

⚡ Other Tests
